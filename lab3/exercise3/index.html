Nawutoot Uzumaki is a ninja from the the Hidden Leaf village. His bestfriend is Sasooke Uchiha who ran away from home to seek vengeance. His goal is to bring him back to Hidden Leaf and become the Hokage. Hokage is the highest title/rank that someone can have. He holds the legendary Nine-Tailed Fox in him which makes him very powerful. His favorite food is Ichiraku Ramen. He has a bright personality, unlike his bestfriend Sasooke Uchiha. During his childhood, he became a member of team 7. His teammates were Sasooke Uchiha, Sakura Haruno, and their sensei Kakashi Hatake.
